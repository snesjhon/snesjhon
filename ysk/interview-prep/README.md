### Daily Routine

**Full-Time (6-8 hours/day)**:
- Morning (3-4 hrs): DSA practice
- Afternoon (2-3 hrs): System Design study
- Evening (30-60 mins): Review and planning

**Part-Time (3-4 hours/day)**:
- Mon/Wed/Fri: 2 hrs DSA + 1 hr SD
- Tue/Thu: 1 hr DSA + 2 hrs SD
- Weekend: Balanced mix + review

### Weekly Check-In

Every Sunday, review:
- DSA: Problems solved, patterns learned, what's confusing?
- System Design: Systems designed, concepts learned, gaps?
- Overall: Hit time targets? Energy level? Adjustments needed?

## ğŸ’¡ Key Principles

1. **Study Both in Parallel**: Don't wait to "finish" one before starting the other
2. **60/40 Time Split**: More time on DSA, but don't neglect System Design
3. **Depth Over Breadth**: Understand 150 problems deeply > know 300 superficially
4. **Active Learning**: Code every solution, draw every architecture
5. **Spaced Repetition**: Revisit problems and systems regularly
6. **Consistency > Intensity**: 6 focused hours > 12 distracted hours
