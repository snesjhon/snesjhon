## Quick Reference Guide

### When asked "Tell me about a time..."
1. **Identify the domain**: Technical? Collaboration? Architecture?
2. **Check relevant question page**: Find your best example
3. **Use the talking points**: Each experience has interview-specific notes

### Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Review all question pages

- [ ] Practice talking points for top 5-7 questions
- [ ] Prepare follow-up question responses

## Questions to Ask Them

- What does success look like for this role in the first 6 months?
- How is the engineering team structured? Who would I be working most closely with?
- What's the onboarding process like for new engineers?
- How do engineers grow technically here? Any mentorship or learning budget?
- What's the team's approach to staying current with technology?

---

**Pro tip**: Before each interview, review 3-5 most relevant question pages based on the role (e.g., for a performance-focused role, review [[performance-optimization]] and [[debugging-challenges]]).
